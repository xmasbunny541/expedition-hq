# Success Criteria

## MVP success

- Known agents, tools, routes, hosts, archives, and blockers are represented from the imported census.
- Dashboard shows who is doing what in a read-only way.
- Every major action can produce an event, field report, or artifact.
- No secrets are committed.
- App runs locally without cloud dependency.
- SQLite stores events from manual tests.
- At least one milestone and one badge are visible.
- Agent maturity and activity states are visible without opening raw logs.

## "Little AI guys" success

A glance at Bureau HQ should make these obvious:
- who is awake
- who is dormant
- who is blocked
- who is working on which expedition
- which systems are routes/tools rather than people-like agents
