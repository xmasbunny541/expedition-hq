# Season 0.1 participation feed is graphics-independent

type: learning
observed_at: 2026-05-30T00:20:12-07:00
scope: season-0.1 tracking
source: current implementation 2026-05-30

## Body

Season 0.1 participation tracking should use the read-only /season-participation endpoint, derived from event ledger and Planning Bureau proposal records. Homepage or hubworld graphics can change without becoming the source of truth for tasks, goals, proposals, meaningful actions, XP, or agent participation.

## Impact

Future UI and visual redesigns should consume the endpoint instead of inventing separate tracking state.

## Next Step

Keep approved proposals and Work Queue in their existing Planning Bureau status buckets until August resumes proposal phase work.
